Why Us?

Being leaders in the Animation, Multimedia and VFX training space located in Guwahati, Assam. We take our responsibilities as trainers and career makers very seriously. We set benchmarks in training standards with Responsibility, Competence, Integrity and High Standards.

Best place to enhance artistic knowledge under the guidance of the highly experienced faculty.
